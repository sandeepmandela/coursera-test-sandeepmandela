Welcome to Salon De Paris

Located in the thriving Downtown near Santa Fe, Salon De Paris is both a full service salon and high desert hub for world-class stylist education, uniting opulence with sustainability, and sophistication with hospitality. We are a salon family and we celebrate our connectedness. Our stylists and hosts cooperate in all things to bring you our best service, creating rich collaboration and positive support. Welcome to the family.
